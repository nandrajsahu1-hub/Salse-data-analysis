## KEY FINDINGS

1. **Seasonal Sales Spike:** Monthly sales analysis revealed a significant peak during Q4 (specifically November and December), likely driven by holiday shopping and promotional discounts.
2. **Top-Performing Product Category:** Technology and Electronics emerged as the highest revenue-generating product categories, while Office Supplies had the highest volume of orders.
3. **Regional Revenue Distribution:** The West region generated the maximum sales and profit, whereas the South region showed the lowest performance, indicating a need for targeted marketing there.
4. **Profitability & Discounts:** Analysis showed a negative correlation between high discounts and profit margins; products with over 20% discount often resulted in a net loss.
5. **Sales Forecasting Accuracy:** The Linear Regression model successfully predicted next month's sales with an R-squared score of [Apna Score likhein, e.g., 85%], showing a strong reliable trend for inventory management.
